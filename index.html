E-Bike Testing Bench

About Project

Our project attempts to establish a solution to a problem of slow, manual analysis of tire damage during inspection of e-bikes on a test bench owned by KdG Research Centre. Can image recognition, AI and a lot of cameras help automate the process? We’ve experimented with Intel RealSense depth and color cameras to grab the image feed, OpenCV for image recognition and explored CNNs, TensorFlow for AI-based solution for defect detection. During the presentation we’ll show our findings, obstacles encountered, discuss alternative approaches to the problem and technologies used to ultimately deliver a prototype solution.
